I have always believed in the freedom to create and express myself in many
different shapes and styles ... different "mirrors" reflecting the heart ....
From the beginning, I tried to be true to my identity as a creator, my job being
more visibly technical than musical, in order to maintain "my music" in the
most personal way ... with all its consequences.

"The beauty of the intangible, invisible but present; images without light or
shade or color but crisp and clear music ..."

"Music with images ...... images with music .."

Music has always been and is the engine and the center of my life.
The concept of music for me goes beyond being able to play any music,
reproduce or imitate others ... I've always wanted to create, transform images
and inner emotions with music, no matter the trend or style. I used the
medium to facilitate expression with acoustic instruments, electronics,
computers ...
Rather than being an instrumentalist, I consider myself someone who
composes, creates. I'm more interested atmospheres or tonal harmonic
progressions ... expressiveness rather than technical exhibitionism.
What matters is the music .... My music.

"Free are those who believe and who not copied, and free are those who think, not those who obey. "

Music has been always the center of my life, I studied electronics in an effort to
learn how to produce on the radio ..... and there I discovered my interest in art.
When I was studying the media was not as extensive as it is now, which
encouraged me to seek further information in books and journals and to take
specialized courses ..
I started designing and putting together my first and sound equipment:
speakers, amplifiers, etc ... renting for events and working in various
nightclubs discs.
I have always been interested in electronic design, new materials and
techniques, modifications, redesign, repair and learning new things... making
inventions.
Since 1978 I have had the opportunity to work in Direct TV, radio, studios,
theater, tours, concerts, shows large and small with analogue and digital
equipment, electronic shows .. as part of my choice to be a sound technician.
The important thing is to be building and learning more, taking music as a
motor of life.
